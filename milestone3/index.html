Company Description

At Masochism Inc., we don’t make games — we forge ordeals. Born from the darkest depths of digital torment, our mission is to craft experiences so grueling, so merciless, that only the truly obsessed will dare to endure them. Every level is a labyrinth of suffering, every enemy a cruel joke at your expense, and every victory? A hard-earned hallucination. We are the cult of challenge, the temple of trial, and the last stop before sanity slips away. Because here at Masochism Inc., if you’re not screaming, you’re not trying.
